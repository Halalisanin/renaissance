Frequently Asked Questions

What is Renaissance?

Renaissance is a contemplative space for exploring spirituality, philosophy, and the deep questions of existence. We draw from Eastern traditions, Western thought, and African wisdom to help you live a more examined life.

Do I need a philosophy background?

Not at all. All content is presented in accessible, everyday language. We believe the deep questions belong to everyone, not just academics.

Does Renaissance promote any particular religion?

No. We are non-sectarian and draw wisdom from many traditions. Our goal is to help you think more deeply about life, not to convert you to any specific belief system.
